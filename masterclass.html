Free Trial Process

Assessment: There will be a diagnostic test for first-time enrollees, to know which level is appropriate for them. Parents have the opportunity to freely choose the classes they want the students to have. The child can also choose a lesson he/she wants to begin with as long as the teacher sees it fit.

Evaluation: An evaluation will be conducted every after class to identify how much they have learned in the lesson.

Review: A review will be conducted before the start of the new lesson.

Study Guide: Free study guides, worksheets, flashcards, etc. will be provided, if needed, after the class and they will be sent together with the feedback of the teacher.

Advantages:

  • In this master class, students are not limited to one lesson or one topic per class. If the student wishes to practice for an upcoming test or do free talk they can do so as long as the teacher is notified.

  • All the Micro and Macro skills in learning English will be applied in the master class as much as possible.

  • Free rescheduling of class, no penalty, and classes are consumable.

  • Free books, worksheets, and activities for the lessons.

  • Feedback will be provided after every class.
